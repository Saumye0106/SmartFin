## üîß How AI Works (Simple Explanation)

### Machine Learning Basics
**Think of ML like teaching a child:**
1. **Show Examples:** Give the AI thousands of examples of financial data
2. **Let It Learn:** AI finds patterns in the data
3. **Test Knowledge:** See if AI can make correct predictions on new data
4. **Improve:** AI gets better with more examples

### Types of AI SmartFin Will Use

#### 1. Classification AI
**What it does:** Puts things into categories
**Example:** "Is this expense food, transportation, or entertainment?"

#### 2. Prediction AI
**What it does:** Forecasts future values
**Example:** "What will your financial health score be next month?"

#### 3. Recommendation AI
**What it does:** Suggests actions
**Example:** "Based on users like you, try reducing coffee expenses by $50/month"

#### 4. Natural Language AI
**What it does:** Understands and generates human language
**Example:** Converts "debt-to-income ratio of 0.4" into "You're spending 40% of your income on debt payments, which is higher than recommended"
